Theme: Everyday Life

Make something that eases a daily task — tidy up routines, solve small annoyances, help students or families, or add a little joy to ordinary days. Small impact, real usefulness.

Who should join

Everyone — beginners welcome. Teams of 1–4 people.

Ideas to try

Habit trackers, automations, organizing tools, micro-utilities, accessibility helpers.

Judging

Impact (40%), Creativity (30%), Execution (20%), Demo polish (10%).

Timeline

  • Idea submission: Day 1
  • Build: Day 1–2
  • Demos & judging: Day 3

Quick Submit

Rules & Tips

  1. Keep scope small — polish beats unfinished complexity.
  2. Show a working demo or prototype.
  3. Explain the everyday problem clearly.
  4. Respect intellectual property and open-source licenses.

Judging Criteria

  • Impact (40%) — How meaningful is the improvement?
  • Creativity (30%) — Unique or clever solutions.
  • Execution (20%) — Working prototype & usability.
  • Demo (10%) — Clarity and presentation.
